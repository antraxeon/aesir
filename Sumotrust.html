    Flexible Savings & Investment Accounts

    Based on your need and convenience, use any of our account types
    to save and grow your finances.

    1

    Main Savings

    Automatically choose to save either on daily, weekly or
    monthly intervals and earn 10% interest per annum.
    Withdrawals here are restricted to only withdrawal dates or
    the exact date you choose to withdraw.

    2

    Fixed Savings

    Securely lock away your money for a long period of time ( up
